## Eval impressions

**```GLM 5 Turbo``` produced the best results in terms of content, speed, and quality.**

**```GLM 5.2``` produced comparable quality content but suffered from overthinking which hindered its response speed.**

**```GLM 5 Turbo``` continuations were shorter than those of ```GLM 5.2```**

**Overall, both models performed really well with ```GLM 5.2``` outputs being slightly better in terms of quality and content but not in terms of speed.**
